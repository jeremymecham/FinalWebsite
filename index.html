A bit about me, Jeremy Mecham

Hello! My name is Jeremy Mecham. I am a Computer Science Major attending Weber state University. I already have a BS in UX design from Utah State University, but I wanted to dive deeper into programming and computer science. I have done a lot of Python, some web development languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), and some SQL (MySQL and Oracle). I have always enjoyed computers and frequently modded Android phones back in the day by adding custom operating systems and boosting performance through overclocking. My personal hobbies include playing video games, playing pickleball, and generally doing anything active.
